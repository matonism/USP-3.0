Ultimate Summer Party 2020
Its time for the 4th installment of the Ultimate Summer Party series. USP 4. 2020 edition. USP 4? Or USP '20? Lets just call it USP 4/20. You can expect Ultimate Summer Party™ 4/20 to deliver on the same experience you've come to love. For many of us, this is our one day a year to shed the shackles of everyday responsibility and celebrate being alive with good music, great competition, and beautiful people. John Jackson WILL be there. Come join us for the event of the year and most likely your life. Let us know what you want in your USP experience and we'll make it happen. And as always, cowards are not welcome





There will be multiple open grills for a BYOM (bring your own meat) cookout. The venue includes a 300 foot private pond open for unlimited swimming, fishing, and canoeing. The event concludes with a bonfire-lit closing ceremony for those who truly conquer the day. Unless you're a tool and don't put the fire out
